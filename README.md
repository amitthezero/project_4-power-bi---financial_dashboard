# BUSINESS PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Business Performance Analytics Dashboard** developed to analyze and monitor critical business KPIs across multiple operational domains including:

- **Finance Analysis**
- **Profit Analysis**
- **Discount Analysis**
- **Manufacturing Efficiency**
- **Country & Product Performance**
- **Segment-wise Profitability**
- **COGS & Margin Tracking**

The dashboard provides a centralized analytical view of business operations, enabling stakeholders to identify profitability drivers, discount impacts, manufacturing efficiency gaps, and revenue contribution across products, countries, and customer segments.

---

# Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to answer key business questions such as:

- Which customer segments generate the highest profit?
- How are discounts impacting profit margins?
- Which products are operationally efficient?
- Which countries contribute most to revenue and COGS?
- How does manufacturing cost influence profitability?
- Which products should be prioritized for strategic growth?
- How are monthly financial metrics trending over time?

# DASHBOARD PAGE 2 — PROFIT ANALYSIS

## Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue:** `$118.7M`
- **Total Profit:** `$16.9M`
- **Profit Margin:** `14.2%`
- **Total COGS:** `$101.8M`

---

# Business Questions Answered

### Which customer segment is most profitable?

### Insights

| Segment | Profit Margin |
|---|---|
| Channel Partners | 73.1% |
| Midmarket | 27.7% |
| Government | 21.7% |
| Small Business | 9.8% |
| Enterprise | -3.1% |

### Business Conclusion

- **Channel Partners** deliver extremely high margins.
- **Enterprise segment is loss-making**, requiring pricing or operational review.

---

###  How is COGS distributed across countries and time?

### Insights

- United States has the highest operational cost contribution.
- COGS intensity increases significantly during high sales months.
- Canada and France also contribute heavily to operational expenses.

### Business Conclusion

Cost optimization initiatives should prioritize high-cost regions.
